# 📟 YOU-Exchange (YOU-X)
> **Trade your habits. Grow your Equity. Don’t let your life crash.**

YOU-X is a high-octane habit-tracking terminal built for those who treat personal growth like a high-stakes trading floor. Built with **Python**, **Streamlit**, and **SQLite**, it transforms your daily discipline into a volatile personal stock market.

---

## 📉 The Core Metaphor
In this ecosystem, **you are the Hedge Fund Manager.** 
- **Habits are Stocks:** Every habit is a ticker symbol (e.g., `$GYM`, `$CODE`, `$SLEEP`).
- **Net Worth is Consistency:** Your total portfolio value is the sum of your daily discipline.
- **The Market is Live:** Hit your habit, and your stock "closes green." Skip it, and the market "gaps down," liquidating your gains.

---

## 🔥 Key Features

### 📈 Real-Time Ticker Tape
A live, scrolling Wall Street ticker at the top of the terminal showing current prices, directions (↗/↘), and percentage gains or losses for every habit in your life.

### ⚙️ The Price Engine
The market reacts exponentially to your behavior using data science logic:
- ✅ **Trade Completion:** Triggers a **+5.2% gain** + a "Streak Multiplier." The longer you stay consistent, the faster the price climbs.
- ❌ **Trade Miss:** Triggers an instant **-20.0% Market Crash**. One bad day can wipe out a week of gains.

### 🛡️ Status & Volatility
- **Blue Chip Status:** Maintain a high consistency average to become a "Blue Chip" asset, signifying prime reliability.
- **Sub-Prime:** High volatility (on-and-off behavior) drops your status and crashes your "Credit Score."

### ⚔️ Market Administration
- **IPO (Initial Personal Offering):** Launch new habits directly from the terminal.
- **Delisting:** Liquidate and remove assets that no longer serve your portfolio goals.

*   **Logic:** Pandas & NumPy

---

## 🎮 How to Trade

1.  **Morning Check-in:** Open the terminal to see your overnight "Net Worth."
2.  **Execute Trades:** 
    *   Complete your real-life habit (e.g., hit the gym or write 100 lines of code).
    *   Click the **COMPLETE** button on the Trading Floor to see the price surge.
3.  **Log Failures:** If you miss a habit, you **must** log a **MISS** to reflect the market crash. This builds honesty and data accuracy.
4.  **Analyze Action:** Scroll down to the **Market Analysis** chart to see your historical price action. Are you a stable "Blue Chip" investment or a high-risk "Pump and Dump"?

---

## 🧪 The Price Formula
For the data-driven users, the price movement is calculated mathematically to reward long-term consistency:

$$Price_{new} = Price_{old} \times (1 + (Streak \times 0.05)) - (Miss \times 0.20)$$

*   **The Streak Multiplier:** Every consecutive day you hit a habit increases the growth percentage. Consistency literally compounds.
*   **The Market Correction:** A single "Miss" triggers an instant 20% drop, simulating the difficulty of regaining lost momentum.

## 🛠️ Market Administration

*   **Delist Asset:** If a habit is no longer relevant to your goals, liquidate it to keep your terminal clean.
